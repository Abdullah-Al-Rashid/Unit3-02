Animal Farm

A dictator is a ruler who has complete power over a country, imposing his leadership over the public. In George Orwell’s Animal Farm, more than any other character, Napoleon personifies the idea that power corrupts. Throughout the novel, Napoleon is a cruel leader who is abusive and barbaric towards his comrades. In addition, Napoleon betrays his own species (Snowball). Although Napoleon emerges as the leader of Animal Farm after Old Major’s death, he is the most loathsome of all characters in Animal Farm. Napoleon is the most loathsome of all the animals because he is selfish, power-hungry, and wicked.
